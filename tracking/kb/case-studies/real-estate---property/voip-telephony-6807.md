- **Estimated effort:** 26 hrs (1232: 26h)

## Scope of work

### Planning and Requirement Analysis

**Problem / context:**

This phase involves understanding the requirements for the upgrade and preparing a roadmap for implementation.

**Strategy:**

Conduct meetings with staff to assess current usage and gather feedback on desired features.

**Objectives:**

Define clear upgrade goals, timelines, and responsibilities.

**General requirements:**

Access to current 3CX configurations, hardware specifications, and a backup of current system settings.

**Reporting & monitoring:**

Regular updates to be provided throughout the planning process and via documented meeting minutes.

**Assumptions:**

All existing hardware and software configurations are compatible with the new version.

### Implementation and Testing

**Problem / context:**

This phase includes the actual upgrade process and testing of the new system.

**Strategy:**

Adopt a staged implementation approach to gradually upgrade features and test each stage before full deployment.

**Objectives:**

Upgrade to 3CX version 20 with minimal disruption to services.

**General requirements:**

Backup of existing system settings and data prior to commencing the upgrade.

**Reporting & monitoring:**

Monitor the upgrade process closely, documenting issues as they arise for immediate resolution.

**Assumptions:**

Internet connectivity and access to the 3CX server are available throughout the upgrade process.

### Training and Support

**Problem / context:**

Providing users with necessary training on the new features and ongoing support.

**Strategy:**

Facilitate hands-on training sessions and provide user manuals for reference.

**Objectives:**

Ensure all users are comfortable and proficient with the new system.

**General requirements:**

Access to training materials and resources for users.

**Reporting & monitoring:**

Gather user feedback to fine-tune training content as necessary.

**Assumptions:**

All personnel are available for training sessions during the scheduled times.
