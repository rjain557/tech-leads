## Scope of work

### Preparation and Planning

**Problem / context:**

This phase includes gathering requirements, assessing current systems, and planning the upgrade process to ensure a smooth transition.

**Strategy:**

Develop a detailed timeline and checklist for the upgrade process, involving all stakeholders.

**Objectives:**

To prepare for a seamless upgrade process without disrupting current operations.

**General requirements:**

Availability of necessary resources including personnel and hardware during the upgrade.

**Assumptions:**

All current systems are functioning properly and are compatible with version 20.

### Upgrade Deployment

**Problem / context:**

This phase focuses on the actual implementation of the upgrade, including installation and configuration of version 20.

**Strategy:**

Utilize a phased approach to implement the upgrade, starting with a pilot test before a full rollout.

**Objectives:**

To successfully upgrade the system while minimizing downtime.

**General requirements:**

Access to the servers and network settings during off-peak hours to minimize disruption.

**Reporting & monitoring:**

Track upgrade progress with daily check-ins and provide updates to stakeholders.

**Assumptions:**

All prerequisites for the upgrade have been met and verification is complete.

### Post-Upgrade Support and Training

**Problem / context:**

This phase involves providing support to users following the upgrade and training them on new features.

**Strategy:**

Conduct training sessions and provide ongoing support based on user feedback.

**Objectives:**

To enhance user adoption and ensure that all features are fully understood.

**General requirements:**

Creation of training materials and resources for user guidance.

**Assumptions:**

Users will require training for new functionalities introduced in version 20.
