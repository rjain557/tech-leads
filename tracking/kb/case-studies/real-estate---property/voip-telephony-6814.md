## Scope of work

### Upgrade Planning and Assessment

**Problem / context:**

This initial phase involves assessing the current system and planning for the upgrade process, ensuring minimal disruption to ongoing operations.

**Strategy:**

Conduct detailed system analysis followed by a staged rollout of the upgrade process.

**Objectives:**

To develop a comprehensive upgrade plan that encompasses all necessary assessments and resource requirements.

**General requirements:**

Adequate backup systems are in place to mitigate the risk of data loss during the upgrade.

**Reporting & monitoring:**

Weekly updates will be provided during the planning phase, summarizing progress and challenges.

**Assumptions:**

It is assumed that all current software licenses are up to date and that the necessary hardware meets the requirements for version 20.

### Implementation & Configuration

**Problem / context:**

The actual upgrade will be executed during this phase, with configuration adjustments made to optimize the new version''s features.

**Strategy:**

Implement the upgrade during off-peak hours to minimize impact on users.

**Objectives:**

Execute the upgrade without disrupting business operations.

**General requirements:**

Ensure all users are trained on the new features and functionalities.

**Reporting & monitoring:**

Daily logs will be maintained to track the implementation progress and any issues encountered.

**Assumptions:**

All contingency plans are ready and any foreseeable issues addressed.

### Testing and Validation

**Problem / context:**

This phase is focused on testing the upgraded system to validate functionality and performance.

**Strategy:**

Conduct thorough testing including user acceptance tests and performance validation.

**Objectives:**

To ensure all features are functioning as expected and to identify any areas of concern.

**General requirements:**

A rollback plan will be ready in case of critical issues arising during testing.

**Reporting & monitoring:**

Testing results will be documented and shared with stakeholders for transparency.

**Assumptions:**

Users will actively partake in testing to provide valuable feedback.

### Deployment and Training

**Problem / context:**

Final deployment of 3CX version 20 across all teams along with user training on new features.

**Strategy:**

Host training sessions and provide support for any specific user queries related to the new version.

**Objectives:**

To smoothly transition all users to the new system and enhance their understanding of its capabilities.

**General requirements:**

Training materials and user manuals will be available before the training sessions.

**Reporting & monitoring:**

Sign-in sheets and feedback forms will be collected to assess training effectiveness.

**Assumptions:**

All users will have received prior notifications about the upgrade.
