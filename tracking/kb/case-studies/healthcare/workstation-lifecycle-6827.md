## Scope of work

### Initial Upgrade Phase

**Problem / context:**

This phase focuses on the upgrade of 18 eligible computers over a weekend, utilizing MyRemote for the in-place upgrade. The goal is to have most computers upgraded by the end of the weekend.

**Strategy:**

Upgrade all eligible computers during off-peak hours to minimize disruption. Technical staff will be on hand to troubleshoot and address any immediate issues.

**Objectives:**

Successfully upgrade as many computers as possible to Windows 11 and troubleshoot common issues immediately.

**General requirements:**

All computers must have a stable internet connection, and users should be informed about the upgrade schedule in advance.

**Reporting & monitoring:**

The success of each upgrade will be monitored in real-time via MyRemote, with reports generated post-upgrade for review.

**Assumptions:**

It is assumed that all eligible computers are currently functioning and meet the system requirements for Windows 11.

### Post-Upgrade Support Phase

**Problem / context:**

This phase will address any computers that did not upgrade successfully during the first phase. Approximately 50% of computers are expected to require additional support.

**Strategy:**

Each computer will be assessed individually to determine the reason for the failure and appropriate fix.

**Objectives:**

Identify and resolve issues for computers that failed to upgrade in Phase 1, and provide updated devices to users where required.

**General requirements:**

Technical support staff will be available to assist users on a case-by-case basis as needed.

**Reporting & monitoring:**

Progress updates will be provided daily during this phase, highlighting successes and obstacles encountered.

**Assumptions:**

It is assumed that users are available to assist with troubleshooting during business hours following the weekend upgrade.
