## Scope of work

### Planning and Assessment

**Problem / context:**

This phase involves assessing the current system and planning the upgrade process, including risk assessment and defining success criteria.

**Strategy:**

Conduct interviews with system users and IT to gather feedback on current system usage and areas for improvement.

**Objectives:**

To determine the readiness of the current system for the upgrade.

**General requirements:**

A review of the current 3CX system to identify any potential issues that may arise during the upgrade.

**Reporting & monitoring:**

Progress reports will be provided weekly to ensure all planning activities remain on schedule.

**Assumptions:**

The current infrastructure meets the necessary requirements for the upgrade, and all stakeholders are available for timely decision-making.

### Upgrade Preparation

**Problem / context:**

This phase will focus on preparing the environment for the upgrade, including data backups and hardware checks.

**Strategy:**

Set up a staging environment for testing the upgrade process and identify any necessary adjustments.

**Objectives:**

To achieve a seamless transition with no data loss during the upgrade.

**General requirements:**

Ensuring all critical data is backed up before the upgrade.

### Upgrade Execution

**Problem / context:**

This phase involves executing the upgrade from version 18 to version 20, ensuring minimal disruption to business operations.

**Strategy:**

Carry out the upgrade during off-peak hours to minimize impact.

**Objectives:**

To complete the upgrade within the planned downtime and verify system functionality post-upgrade.

**Reporting & monitoring:**

Daily update sessions during the upgrade process to address any issues immediately.

### Review and Training

**Problem / context:**

After the upgrade, this phase focuses on reviewing the upgrade outcomes and conducting training sessions for users.

**Strategy:**

Conduct training workshops and gather feedback from users to improve usability.

**Objectives:**

To ensure users are comfortable with the new version and features of 3CX.

**Reporting & monitoring:**

Collect feedback from users on the new system for evaluation.
