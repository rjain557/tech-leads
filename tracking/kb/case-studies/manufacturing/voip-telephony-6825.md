## Scope of work

### Upgrade Planning and Preparation

**Problem / context:**

This phase includes an in-depth assessment of the current 3CX system, identification of dependencies, and preparation for the upgrade process.

**Strategy:**

Conduct a thorough analysis of the existing configuration and user requirements.

**Objectives:**

Assess the current system and prepare for the upgrade.

**General requirements:**

Access to the current system and configuration documents.

**Reporting & monitoring:**

Weekly updates will be provided to the manufacturer on the preparation status.

**Assumptions:**

The current environment is stable, and all configurations are documented.

### Upgrade Execution

**Problem / context:**

This phase involves the actual upgrade of the 3CX system from version 18 to version 20, including data migration and configuration adjustments.

**Strategy:**

Implement the upgrade during off-peak hours to minimize disruption.

**Objectives:**

Successfully complete the upgrade and ensure all services are operational.

**General requirements:**

Coordination with the manufacturer’s IT for service outages during the upgrade.

**Reporting & monitoring:**

Immediate reporting of any issues encountered during the upgrade.

**Assumptions:**

All user data is backed up and easily accessible.

### Training and Support

**Problem / context:**

This phase includes training for the manufacturer staff on any new features and ongoing support for any issues that arise post-upgrade.

**Strategy:**

Schedule training sessions and provide resources for self-learning.

**Objectives:**

Ensure all users are comfortable with the new system features.

**General requirements:**

Training materials and session schedules to be provided prior.

**Reporting & monitoring:**

Feedback will be collected after training to assess effectiveness.

**Assumptions:**

Staff are available for training sessions.
