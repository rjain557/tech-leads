# Sophos SF V-2C4 Virtual Firewall & Edge Appliance Deployment

- **Industry:** Financial Services
- **Service theme:** network-security
- **Engagement date:** 2026-03-23
- **Estimated effort:** 33 hrs (1232: 21h, 1236: 12h)

## Client context

The firm is a dually registered Investment Adviser 
(RIA) and Broker-Dealer regulated by the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) and 
the Financial Industry Regulatory Authority (FINRA). As a regulated financial institution 
handling nonpublic personal information (NPI), personally identifiable information (PII), and 
customer financial data, the firm is subject to the following compliance frameworks, each of 
which mandates network-layer security controls:
• SEC Regulation S-P (2024 Amendments) — Privacy of Consumer Financial 
Information and Safeguarding Customer Information
• SEC Regulation S-ID — Identity Theft Red Flag Rules
• FINRA Rule 4370 — Business Continuity Plans and Emergency Contact Information
• FINRA Rule 3110 — Supervision (supervisory continuity during disruptions)

• FINRA Cybersecurity Guidance — Annual Regulatory and Examination Priorities
• FTC Safeguards Rule (16 CFR Part 314) — Financial Institution Information Security 
Requirements
• HIPAA Security Rule — Where the firm handles protected health information
• SOC 2 Trust Services Criteria — Service organization controls for data security
• PCI-DSS 4.0 — Payment card data handling requirements
• GDPR — Where applicable to EU data subjects
This SOW covers the supply, deployment, configuration, and ongoing management of a 
Technijian-provided Edge Appliance 16GB with VMware ESXi hypervisor hosting a Sophos 
SF V-2C4 Virtual Firewall to establish a compliant, enterprise-grade network security 
perimeter for the firm’s 15-user environment. Technijian will supply all hardware, hypervisor 
software, and virtual appliance licensing as a fully managed solution. This engagement 
directly addresses critical control requirements across all applicable compliance
frameworks and closes identified gaps from the firm’s BCP Compliance Review (March 6, 
2026).
