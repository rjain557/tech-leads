# My Security — Outreach template

**Frame:** Company hiring first/solo security person, or a JD that asks one hire to provide 24/7 coverage (impossible → MSSP need). Also post-breach or mid-cyber-insurance-renewal signals. Pitch: EDR/XDR + SIEM + email sec + identity + DLP, U.S.-based 24/7 SOC, 15-min IR SLA, compliance mapping for HIPAA/SOC 2/PCI/CMMC/NIST.

## Subject variants

1. Re: your {{role}} posting
2. About the {{role}} role at {{company}}
3. 24/7 coverage question — {{role}} JD

## Touch 1 — Day 0

> Hi {{contact_first_name}},
>
> Saw the {{role}} posting. The "{{jd_quote}}" line caught me — asking one hire to own EDR, incident response, vuln management, and the compliance layer tends to mean coverage gaps at 2am and burnout by month 18.

>
> Worth 15 min before they start, so the stack is right?
>

## Service-specific DO

- If JD asks for 24/7 coverage from one hire → call out the math directly (one person ≠ 24/7)
- Reference the specific EDR/SIEM if the JD names one (CrowdStrike, Sentinel, SentinelOne)
- Lead with IR SLA (15 min) — specific and verifiable

## Service-specific DON'T

- Do NOT fear-sell with breach statistics — the security buyer has heard all of them
- Don't bash in-house security teams; the frame is augmentation, not replacement
- Don't pitch pentesting in touch 1 — it's a different buyer journey
