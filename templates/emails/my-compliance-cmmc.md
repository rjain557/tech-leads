## Service-specific DO

- Use the exact DFARS clause numbers — buyers in this space filter hard on vendor literacy
- Mention GCC High only if there's a signal (ITAR in JD, CUI mentioned, defense vertical)
- Lead with assessment readiness and SPRS score — the two metrics primes actually ask about

## Service-specific DON'T

- Do NOT claim "CMMC-certified" unless Technijian is a Registered Practitioner or C3PAO — this space punishes exaggeration hard
- Don't mix CMMC with generic cybersecurity pitch — buyers here want specialists, not generalists
