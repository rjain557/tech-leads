# My SEO — Outreach template

**Frame:** SMB hiring first/solo marketer expected to own SEO + content + ads, OR company with flat/declining organic traffic who's not showing up in ChatGPT/Perplexity (AI search). Pitch: unlimited-hours SEO + content + video + AI search optimization + 250+ syndication + 300+ PR placements, tiered $500-$1500/mo.

## Subject variants

1. Re: your {{role}} posting
2. About the {{role}} JD at {{company}}
3. Organic traffic question for {{company}}

## Touch 1 — Day 0

> Hi {{contact_first_name}},
>
> Saw the {{role}} posting. The "{{jd_quote}}" line tells me {{company}} is asking one marketer to own SEO + content + ads + brand — a setup that usually means one or two of those get dropped within a quarter.
>
> We run a tiered marketing pod ($500-$1500/mo) that covers the SEO + content + AI-search (ChatGPT/Perplexity) side so the new hire can focus on the part only they can do. Unlimited hours, not hourly billed.
>
> Worth 15 min to map it against your actual goals?
>
> {{signature}}

## Touch 2 — Day 4

> {{contact_first_name}} — quick observation on AI search: pages that rank in ChatGPT/Perplexity answers are structured differently than pages that rank in Google — shorter paragraphs, explicit fact claims, citation-friendly format. Auditing existing top content against that format tends to surface 2-3 quick wins before any new content gets written.
>
> Worth a 15-min look?
>
> {{signature}}

## Touch 3 — Day 10

> Did the {{role}} close? If AI search is on the mind, I'll send the audit template we use — no call needed.
>
> {{signature}}

## Service-specific DO

- Lead with AI search (ChatGPT/Perplexity visibility) — it's the differentiator most SEO agencies haven't caught up on
- Name the tiered pricing ($500-$1500/mo) — transparency lands better than "custom quote"
- Call out "unlimited hours" — not hourly billing is distinctive

## Service-specific DON'T

- Don't quote specific ranking lifts you can't defend on a call
- Don't send HTML emails with backlink charts or analytics screenshots — pattern-matches to SEO spam
- Don't pitch backlinks in touch 1 — the spammy-SEO-agency trigger word
