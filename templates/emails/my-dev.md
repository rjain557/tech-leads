## Service-specific DO

- Call out specific legacy tech if named in JD (Classic ASP, WebForms, WCF, .NET Framework)
- Mention Claude Code SDLC explicitly — differentiates from offshore shops
- If JD mentions ERP/CRM integration (Epicor, NetSuite, Salesforce), lead with that as the specific hook

## Service-specific DON'T

- Don't pitch to FAANG or Fortune 500 engineering orgs — exclusion in [services.yml](../../config/services.yml)
- Don't promise specific velocity numbers you can't defend on a call
- Don't bash offshore — buyer may have been burned but respect their past choice
