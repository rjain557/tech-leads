# My AI — Outreach template

**Frame:** Company hiring their first AI engineer or strategist — often with zero engineering support listed. The pitch is "your AI roadmap will stall in PoC purgatory if one person owns strategy + implementation + compliance. We ship production LLM workflows with the compliance layer built in."

## Subject variants

1. Re: your {{role}} posting — one thought
2. About the {{role}} role at {{company}}
3. Saw the {{role}} JD — worth a quick note

## Touch 1 — Day 0

> Hi {{contact_first_name}},
>
> Saw the {{role}} posting. Your JD calls out "{{jd_quote}}" — the part that caught me is it reads like this is the first AI hire with the expectation they'll own strategy, implementation, and {{pain_ref}} all at once.
>
> We ship production LLM workflows (multi-agent, RAG, MCP) in regulated verticals for the same reason your leadership team wants AI: move fast without creating a compliance landmine.
>
> Not suggesting don't hire — suggesting a 15-min call before they land, so they inherit infra instead of building it from scratch in pilot purgatory.
>
> Worth a reply?
>
> {{signature}}

## Touch 2 — Day 4

> {{contact_first_name}} — one concrete thing about first-AI-hire setups: the compliance layer (HIPAA, SOC 2, PCI, GDPR) is what kills most PoCs at the "ship to prod" step. If the scaffolding is in place before they arrive, month one goes into building features instead of reading NIST docs.
>
> Short call worth it?
>
> {{signature}}

## Touch 3 — Day 10

> Did the {{role}} role land, {{contact_first_name}}? If they're still ramping, first 30 days is when our help pays off most — happy to send a one-pager either way.
>
> {{signature}}

## Service-specific DO

- Call out "first AI hire" when the signal supports it — it's the strongest tell in services.yml
- Use AI vocabulary they used (LLM, RAG, MCP, multi-agent) but do not AI-wash the pitch itself
- If the vertical is regulated, lead with compliance-through-implementation as the differentiator

## Service-specific DON'T

- Don't name-drop specific models (Claude, GPT-4) in touch 1 unless the JD does — feels try-hard
- Don't promise "10× productivity" or any metric you can't back up on the call
