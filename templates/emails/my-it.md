**Frame:** SMB hiring a solo/first IT admin. The pitch is not "hire us instead" — it's "before you commit $95K + benefits to one human covering 24/7, see what a fractional MSP covers for less."

## Subject variants

1. Re: your {{role}} search
2. About the {{role}} posting at {{company}}
3. One thought on the {{role}} JD

## Service-specific DO

- Name the "wear many hats" problem without being condescending — most sole-IT admins burn out in 18 months and the buyer knows it
- If the JD mentions multiple offices → lean into "one person supporting N sites is a coverage gap, not a job"
- Reference `pain_signals` from [services.yml](../../config/services.yml): vendor chaos, unpredictable costs, no vCIO

## Service-specific DON'T

- Don't bash in-house IT. The buyer may have a capable admin today — the pitch is augmentation, not replacement
- Don't mention specific tools (CrowdStrike, Datto, ConnectWise) in touch 1 — saves them for touch 2+ or the call
